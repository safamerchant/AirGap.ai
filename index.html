AirGap.ai
Welcome · Sandbox mode

A circuit breaker for
autonomous AI agents.

AirGap.ai is a middleware proxy plus a live control dashboard. It sits between your agent and the outside world, so the agent can act freely on the safe side of the breaker while your real systems stay isolated from anything risky.

How every action is handled
Intercept Every tool call the agent attempts is caught before it executes.
Score Each action is rated against your spend budgets and risk bounds.
Decide Safe actions pass through; anything over the line is held for one-click human approval. The kill switch halts everything instantly.
Where to start
→

Hit Demo the product at the bottom of this page (or Console in the top-right) to open the live control dashboard. That's where you'll watch agent actions get intercepted, scored, and approved or blocked in real time.

Under the hood · what the demo uses
  • Simulated agent traffic. Everything runs in a sandbox — the actions are mock requests, so no real accounts, payments, or systems are ever touched.
  • Action logs are the core data. Each intercepted action carries a risk score, a budget impact, and an approve / deny / pending state that drives the dashboard.
  • Policies you control. Budgets, risk thresholds, and the kill switch decide what passes automatically and what waits for you.
  • Nothing leaves the sandbox. The whole flow is safe to click through end to end.
